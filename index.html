Terms & Conditions

Booking & Payment Terms

1. A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking to reserve your dates. Your booking is not confirmed until the deposit is received.

2. The remaining 50% balance must be paid no later than 30 days before your arrival date. Bookings made within 30 days of arrival require full payment upfront.

3. A $300 AUD refundable security bond is collected at checkout and returned within 7 days of check-out, provided the property is left in good condition.

4. Cancellation policy:

• 90+ days before arrival — Full refund

• 60–89 days — 75% refund or 100% credit valid 12 months

• 30–59 days — 75% credit valid 12 months

• 15–29 days — 50% credit valid 12 months

• Within 14 days — 25% credit valid 12 months

6. Credit conditions: Credits must be used within 12 months of issue. They may be applied to any available dates subject to availability. Credits are not transferable. If new dates cost more, the guest pays the difference. Credits hold no cash value.

6. A 10% discount applies automatically to stays of 9 nights or more. This discount cannot be combined with other promotional codes — the greater discount will apply.

7. Lupe Accommodations reserves the right to cancel a booking in the event of unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, a full refund will be issued.

7. Guests are responsible for any damage to the property during their stay. Costs exceeding the bond will be invoiced separately.

8. Check-in is from 3:00 PM. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. Late check-out or early check-in may be arranged on request, subject to availability.
